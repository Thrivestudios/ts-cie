"It's What They Do"

The explosion of apps is bringing new and interesting uses of our network to consumers. Digital Heroism – the “better versions of ourselves” that are made possible by immediate access to anything we need – is a key trend we are monitoring to strengthen the AT&T Brand.

iPhone Drives Highest Data Consumption

Heavy Data Users on iPhone are the biggest consumers of wireless data. Our Heavy Data Users consider their use to be typical, and rarely consider steps to manage their use of the network.
